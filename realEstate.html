Where Should Your Money Go?

  1. Single Family
    • If you have a family.
    • If you want your own space.
    • If you have money to put as a down payment.
    • If you want to own your own house.
  2. Small Unit
    • If you have a family.
    • If you want some space from lots of other people.
    • If you do not want to put a large amount of money down.
  3. Large Unit
    • If you do not have a family.
    • If you want lots of neighbors close or if they do not bother you.
    • If you do not have money to put down on a house payment.
    • These are also easier to find closer to downtown in cities.

Types of Homes

Single Family

A single family home typically holds one family. Typically has 1-2 bathrooms, and 2-5 bedrooms.

Small Unit

A multi unit property that typically holds 4 or less units. Normally 1-4 bedrooms and 1-2 bathrooms. Can be larger units depending on size.

Large Unit

A multi unit property that holds more than 4 units. These are typically smaller and are meant to hold 1-3 people unlike the others that can hold families. Normally has 1-3 bedrooms and 1-2 bathrooms.
